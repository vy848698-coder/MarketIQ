FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything founders usually ask before working with us.

? How long does SEO take to show results?
SEO is a compounding investment. Most clients see early movement in 6–8 weeks (rankings, traffic) and meaningful lead growth by month 3–6, depending on competition and your starting point. We share progress in a live dashboard from week one — no waiting until month six to see what's happening.
? What's your PPC management fee?
Our PPC management is a flat monthly fee based on ad spend and complexity — typically 10–15% of spend, with transparent reporting and no hidden markups on your ad budget. We'll quote an exact figure on your free strategy call once we understand your goals.
? Do you work with small businesses?
Absolutely — local businesses and startups are our specialty. We build flexible plans that fit a smaller budget and focus spend where it drives the most return, so you're never paying for bloat you don't need.
? Do you require long-term contracts?
No long lock-ins. We work on flexible, performance-based growth plans — most clients stay because of results (94% retention), not because a contract forces them to.
? What industries do you serve?
We have deep playbooks for solar, real estate, healthcare, education, manufacturing and local businesses — but our data-driven process works across almost any industry. If you sell to customers online, we can help you grow.
