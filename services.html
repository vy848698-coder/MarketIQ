FAQ

Service questions

The details founders ask about most before getting started.

? How long does SEO take?
Early movement (rankings and traffic) usually shows in 6–8 weeks, with meaningful lead growth by month 3–6 — it depends on your competition and starting point. You'll see progress in a live dashboard from week one, so you're never waiting in the dark.
? What is included in PPC management?
Everything to make paid ads profitable: account restructure, keyword and audience targeting, ad creative, landing pages, conversion tracking, and continuous A/B testing — plus transparent reporting with no markup on your ad spend.
? Do you create content?
Yes — content is core to what we do. Blog and SEO articles, ad creative, social posts, reels and video scripts, email copy and landing-page copywriting, all mapped to your funnel and brand voice.
? Is there a minimum contract?
We work on flexible, performance-based plans with no long lock-ins. We typically recommend a 3-month runway so strategies have time to compound — but most clients stay because of results, not contracts (94% retention).
? How often will I get reports?
You get a live dashboard updated in real time, plus a clear monthly review call covering leads, costs and ROI. Growth-plan clients also get weekly check-ins with their dedicated strategist.
